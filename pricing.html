Frequently Asked Questions

What broker can I use?

The EA works with any MT5 broker. We recommend brokers with tight spreads on Gold and NAS100 for best performance.

What's the difference between Razor and Power?

Razor uses 2 high probability entry levels while Power uses 3. More levels means more trading opportunities. Both trade Gold and NAS100 with the same precision.

Is it prop firm friendly?

Yes. The EA is designed with prop firm rules in mind. Low drawdown, consistent risk management, and no martingale or grid strategies.

How much capital do I need?

We recommend a minimum of £500 to start, but the EA works with any account size. Risk is calculated as a percentage of your balance.
