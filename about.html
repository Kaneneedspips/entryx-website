Our Story

Why We Built EntryX

I got into trading the same way most people do. YouTube videos at 2am, thinking I'd figured something out. Spent years staring at charts, missing out on life, stressing over every candle.

Don't get me wrong, I was profitable. Just about. Small gains here and there but nothing life changing. And the time it took to get those gains? Mental. Waking up for London open, trading all morning, then spending the rest of the day wondering if I should have held longer or closed sooner.

I just wanted my time back.

Started looking at automation. Bought a few EAs and honestly, all garbage. Nice looking backtests that completely fell apart in live markets. Vendors who vanished after payment. Strategies that only worked when the stars aligned. The industry is full of it.

So I partnered with some freelance MQL5 developers and we built our own. Took over a year. Not using the free demo data everyone else uses, but 5+ years of premium tick data from proper institutional providers. Tested it through multiple market cycles. COVID crash. The recovery. Rate hikes. All of it.

The goal was simple. Something I could trust to run while I got on with my life. No more waking up for sessions. No more staring at charts all day. Just set it up, let it do its thing, and check in when I want to.

That's EntryX. It's what I run on my own money, every single day. The live journal on this site? That's my capital. We use what we sell.
