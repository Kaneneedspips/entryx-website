The EA & Strategy

What if the EA stops working?

+

No trading strategy works forever without adaptation. Markets evolve, and so do we. Our development team continuously monitors performance and releases updates to keep the strategy optimized for current market conditions.

The core strategy has been backtested over 5+ years of market data including multiple market cycles, crashes, and recovery periods. It's designed to be robust, not over-fitted to specific conditions. That said, past performance never guarantees future results - that's just the reality of trading.

What's the difference between Razor and Power?

+

Both EAs use the same core strategy - identifying high probability price levels where the market is likely to reverse or continue. The difference is in how many levels they trade.

Razor trades 2 HP levels - fewer trades, more selective entries. Great if you want a conservative approach.

Power trades 3 HP levels - more opportunities, same risk management. This is what we run on our live account.
